What is UWIN?

The University of Wyoming Innovation Network (UWIN), is a hub of student innovation with multiple locations on campus. Our spaces provide an inspiring environment for creative, collaborative, and entrepreneurial projects. From advanced 3D printing to sewing, embroidery, and vinyl cutting, UWIN offers the technology and equipment to bring your ideas to life.


Discover a world of possibilities at UWIN. Engage in multimedia printing, electronics, CNC milling, and Laser Cutting. Explore virtual reality, utilize laptops with CAD and modeling software, and access a wide range of craft supplies, robotics, circuit, and STEM kits for K-12 instruction. UWIN is open to students, faculty, staff, and the public, creating a vibrant community where innovation thrives and boundaries are pushed. Join us and unleash your creative potential at the University of Wyoming Innovation Network.

Crafts, Vinyl, Laser Engraving, 3D Printing, Textiles, Multi-Media Printing

The CSIC is UWIN's flagship location, first opening its doors in 2017. The CSIC holds technology ranging from rapid prototyping equipment like 3D printers and laser engraving to advanced arts and crafts such as stained glass stations and sewing textiles. Request a tour and see what you can make today! Located in Coe Library.
